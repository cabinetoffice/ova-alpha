Prove veteran identity to continue

Apply for a veteran card

To help someone apply for a veteran card you will need to assist them in proving their identity online. This will involve answering some security questions.

You will need:

  • their passport information
  • answers to questions about their financial details, such as mortgages and loans
  • their addresses for the past 5 years

Prove veteran status for access to services

If you are assisting a veteran to prove their veteran status only, you will still need to prove their identity.

You will need:

  • a picture of their passport
  • a picture of their driving licence
  • their addresses for a period of 5 years
