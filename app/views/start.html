Before you start

If you're signing in to the service for the first time, you'll need:

  • a Gov.UK Sign In account - if you do not have an account, you can create one when you use the service
  • your driving license number, or
  • a valid UK passport

We need these to prove who you are.

You'll also need to tell us:

  • your Service Number
  • the service you served in
  • the year you left the service

We need this so we know you served in HM Armed Forces.

You may be asked some questions to help us find your service record. We won't store or share your answers.

Other ways to apply

You can also register by post.

The online service is also available in Welsh (ar gael yn Gymraeg).
