What you'll need

We need to ask you some details about yourself and the information you are requesting. This is to ensure that only veterans or people who have permission to access their records use this service.

Verifying your identity

You can do this by:

  • using the service on a smart phone or tablet - you'll be able to take a photo with your device's camera later
  • taking a photot witha digital camera, smartphone or tablet and transferring it to your computer - for example, by emailing it to yourself
  • using a scanner and uploading a scan from your computer

You can choose one of the following ID documents to upload a copy of:

  • your UK photocard driving licence, showing your home address
  • the photo page of your passport

Or - any of the following, showing your name and home address, dated within the last three months:

  • utility bill
  • council tax bill
  • bank statement
  • a letter from a government department (for example, HMRC or DWP)

If you cannot upload copies of your documents, you will need to apply by post instead (opens in a new tab)

Verifying your own veteran status

If you've served in the armed forces, we'll ask you for the following details to help us find your information:

  • your service number
  • your enlistment and discharge dates

Verifying someone else's veteran status

You'll need to upload proof that you have the authority to verify this person's status. We'll ask you to upload either:

  • the person's written consent from you to access their information
  • a court order (for example, power of attorney) permitting the release of the information requested
  • evidence that you work for one of the approved services that are authorised to verify a veteran's status

If you cannot any of these, you will need to apply by post instead (opens in a new tab)

We'll ask you for the following details to help us find their information:

  • their service number
  • details of which service(s) they served with

If you cannot provide either of these, we'll still process your request but it might take us longer to find your information,
