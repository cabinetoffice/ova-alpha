Verify that someone is a veteran

Use this service to:

  • Verify that someone has served in HM armed forces

You can use this service to confrim veteran status in order to gain access to services that only ex-services personnel are eligable for.

You will need:

  • service number and dates
  • passport details
  • a picture or scan of a passport or driving licence
  • consent from veteran to request this information

The online service is also available in Welsh (Cymraeg)

You cannot register for this service if you are in the UK illegally
