Prove your identity with a GOV.UK account

You can currently only prove your identity with a your GOV.UK account if you have a UK passport.

  • If your passport has expired, you can still use it to prove your identity up until 18 months after the expiry date

What you need to do

It will take about 10 minutes to prove your identity this way

You will need to create a GOV.UK account first, or sign in if you already have one.
We will then ask you for your current address and passport details.
We will also ask you some security questions that only you should know the answers to.
These could be questions about:

  • your mobile phone contract
  • your bank account
  • any credit card, loans or mortgages yiu have

We will use the information you give us to make sure that you are who you say you are.
