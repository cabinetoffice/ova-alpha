Apply for a veteran card

Use this service to:

  • Apply for a veteran card

You can do this for yourself, or on behalf of another living person if you have their permission.

You can use this service if you are (or the person you're requesting information about is):

  • A former member of the armed forces
  • Someone who is authorised to verify a former member of the armed forces or has their permission to do so

The online service is also available in Welsh (Cymraeg)

You cannot register for this service if you are in the UK illegally
