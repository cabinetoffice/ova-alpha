Apply for a veteran card

Use this service to:

  • Verify that you have served in HM Armed Forces
  • Apply for a veteran card

You can use a veteran card to receive the recognition, support and benefits that only veterans are eligible for.

For more information on the support and benefits available visit Veterans Gateway

What you need to apply

To verify your identity you'll need:

  • a GOV.UK Sign In account

To verify your service:

  • your service number
  • the year you enlisted
  • the year you left service

To receive your digital and/or physical card:

  • an email address
  • a postal address to send the card to
