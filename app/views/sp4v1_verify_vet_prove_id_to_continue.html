Prove veteran status for access to services

To prove veteran status you need to prove their identity.

You will need:

  • a picture of their passport
  • a picture of their driving licence

Apply for a veteran card

To prove identity for a veteran card online, you will need:

You will need:

  • their passport information
  • all addresses for the past 5 years and the dates lived at
  • answers to questions about their finances, such as any mortgages and loans, if applicable
