Verify your identity

You need to verify your identity before we can process your request. You will need to upload a scan or photo of an approved identity document

  • If your passport has expired, you can still use it to prove your identity up until 18 months after the expiry date

If you are using a desktop or laptop, you will need the scan or photo saved as a file on your computer.

If you are using a mobile or tablet, you will be able to upload a photo on the next page.

I can't upload any documents
If you are unable to upload any documents then you can also verify your identity by post or in person at your local drop in centre.
